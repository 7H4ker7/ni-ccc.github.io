NI-CCC

nic: [in czech meaning nothing] a notion so simple, thus with an unlimited meaning [x]

NICCC is a study course that takes place at the Czech Technical University in Prague and it’s in collaboration with many other institutions. The aim of this course is to create a space that connects people from different fields of interest — a merge of IT, architecture and urbanism and its connection with research or art of any kind of format. This platform allows for interdisciplinary projects to become reality — big screen visualisation, physical installations or projects with practically any kind of focus. At its core lays the communication between all participants — people with different sets of knowledge, skills and most of all different fields of interest. This individuality is the base for the birth of unique projects, which are being evolved in collaborations with the externists, who may guide or bring their expertise into the project itself.
